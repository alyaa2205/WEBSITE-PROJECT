HEALTH AND WELLNESS

Date: 25 January 2025 - 1 February 2025

Time: 10:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.

Venue: Dewan Aspirasi UiTM Dungun

Join us for Health and Wellness Week, a week-long event dedicated to improving your overall well-being.

Event Highlights:

  • Fitness Workshops: From beginner to advanced classes.
  • Wellness Talks: Topics include mental health, nutrition, and healthy habits.
  • Health Screenings: Free assessments and consultations.
  • Mindfulness Sessions: Techniques for stress relief and mental clarity.

Take part in this enriching experience and start your journey to a healthier life!

Directions

Find us easily using the map below or follow the driving directions:

Driving Directions:

From Jalan Kuala Terengganu-Dungun (Route 3), follow signs to Dungun. Once in Dungun, follow signs to UiTM Dungun. Dewan Aspirasi is located within the campus.

    Additional Information:

  • Parking is available on-site at the campus, close to the venue.
  • Public transportation stops are nearby, including Grab and Taxis that can take you to the university campus.
  • Accessible entrances are available for individuals with mobility needs at Dewan Aspirasi and the campus.
